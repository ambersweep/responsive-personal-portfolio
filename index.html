About


I'm a career changer! Living in the Orlando area, the theme park capital of the world, most of my work life has been in the hospitality industry. Being part of upholding the title of 'Most Magical Place On Earth' it is not for the faint hearted.

Now that I'm on my way to completing Thinkful's Software Engineering Program, my prior endeavors braving the world of hospitality have prepped me for being thrown into the deep end of coding.

With my only prior experience with coding being editing HTML layouts on Tumblr and Neopets, I had no idea how vast the coding world was. Its been a wild ride, but I've honestly loved every minute of it. It has made me realize this is where I belong. :)
